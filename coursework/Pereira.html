Career

Alex Pereira, a dynamic force in both mixed martial arts and kickboxing, has continues to dominate the UFC with just his striking skills and limited grappling.

Born and raised in Brazil, Pereira began his combat sports journey in kickboxing, where he quickly rose through the ranks with his devastating knockout power and technical precision. His dominance in the kickboxing world earned him numerous championships and accolades, solidifying his reputation as one of the best in the sport.

In addition to his success in kickboxing, Pereira transitioned to mixed martial arts, where he continued to showcase his formidable striking skills and adaptability inside the cage. Bursting onto the scene in the professional MMA circuit, Pereira wasted no time in making an impact, finishing opponents with his trademark left hook.

Throughout his career, Pereira has seamlessly blended his kickboxing background with his MMA endeavors, utilizing his striking expertise to dismantle opponents from various disciplines. He is the first fighter to become champion of the Middleweight and Light Heavyweight divisions in the UFC.
