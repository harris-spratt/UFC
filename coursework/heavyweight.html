About

The UFC Heavyweight division, characterized by fighters with a weight limit of 265 pounds (120.2 kg), stands as a realm of raw power and sheer force within the mixed martial arts landscape. Unlike other weight classes, the Heavyweight division transcends conventional limitations, showcasing behemoths of unparalleled strength and size. Fights tend to finish via knockout or TKO in this division with small mistakes gettin punished to highest extent.
