About

The UFC Middleweight division, with a weight limit of 185 pounds (83.9 kg), stands as a historic and prestigious weight class within mixed martial arts. Renowned for its rich history and talent, this division has showcased some of the most memorable fights in UFC history. Fighters in the Middleweight division possess a diverse range of skills, blending striking, grappling, and submission techniques to compete at the highest level. With legendary champions like Anderson Silva and emerging stars like Israel Adesanya, the division has consistently delivered thrilling matchups and intense rivalries, captivating fans worldwide.
