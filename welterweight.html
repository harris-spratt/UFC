About

The Welterweight division in the UFC has a weight limit of 170 pounds (77.1 kg).This division if definetly one of the fans favourites. Current champion Leon Edwards is the UK's second only UFC Champion and he won the belt in 2022 defeating Kamaru Usman with a memorabel last round headkick. Kamaru Usman and GSP are often considered the GOATS ( Greatest of all time) in this weight class. GSP changed the sport of MMA displaying incredible offensive wresting and boxing skills.
