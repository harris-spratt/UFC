Career

Islam Makhachev, known for his exceptional grappling skills and tactical brilliance, has carved out an impressive career in the world of mixed martial arts. Bursting onto the scene in the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC), Makhachev quickly garnered attention for his dominant performances and relentless pursuit of victory.

Throughout his journey, Makhachev has showcased remarkable versatility, seamlessly blending striking and grappling to overcome formidable opponents. His strategic approach to combat, coupled with his unwavering determination, has propelled him to the upper echelons of the lightweight division.

Despite facing challenges along the way, including injuries and setbacks, Makhachev has remained steadfast in his pursuit of excellence. Some regard him as a better version of Khabib Nurmagomedov and he does not look to be losing anytime soon. His only loss came early on in his UFC career getting knocked out by Adriano Martins.
