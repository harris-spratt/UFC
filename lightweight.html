About

The Lightweight division in the UFC (Ultimate Fighting Championship) is one of the most competitive weight classes in mixed martial arts (MMA) .The Lightweight division was officially established in the UFC in 2001.The Lightweight division has a weight limit of 155 pounds (70.3 kg). Famous champions and matches include Conor Mcgreogor vs Khabib Nurmagamedov which grossed the highest amount of Pay Per View Buys ever in UFC History. At the current moment the number one ranked fighter in the world Islam Makhachev is the chamnpion and not many can see him losing anytime soon.
